# Processing payments on mobile

Operators can accept payments from customers on-site using the VanMan mobile app. This eliminates the need to chase payments after the job and keeps your cash flow moving.

<StepByStep>
  <Step title="Open the job">
    From the mobile app, navigate to the completed job and tap **Process payment**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose a payment method">
    Select how the customer is paying:

    - **Cash** — Record a cash payment received on-site.
    - **POS** — Record a payment taken through your external POS terminal.
    - **Credit card** — Process a credit card payment directly through the app.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter the amount and confirm">
    Enter the payment amount and tap **Confirm payment**. The payment is recorded against the job and the payment status is updated in real time across mobile and web.
  </Step>
</StepByStep>

<Callout variant="tip">
  Process the payment immediately after completing the move while you are still with the customer. This ensures payment is collected promptly and avoids follow-up calls.
</Callout>

<Callout variant="info">
  All payments recorded on mobile are visible in the job detail panel on the web dashboard. If the job has an associated invoice, the payment status is updated there as well.
</Callout>

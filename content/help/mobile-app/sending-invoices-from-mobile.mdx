# Sending invoices from mobile

Operators can generate invoices and send them to customers directly from the VanMan mobile app. This means you can complete a move, create the invoice, and send it before you leave the site.

<StepByStep>
  <Step title="Open the completed job">
    From the mobile app, navigate to the job that has been completed. Ensure the charges and line items have been entered in the tally section.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Generate the invoice">
    Tap **Create invoice**. VanMan generates an invoice using the customer details and charges from the job. Review the line items, amounts, and tax to make sure everything is correct.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Send to the customer">
    Choose how to deliver the invoice:

    - **SMS** — Sends a link to the invoice via text message.
    - **Email** — Sends the invoice as an email with a PDF attachment or inline view.

    Tap **Send** to deliver the invoice to the customer.
  </Step>
</StepByStep>

<Callout variant="info">
  If you have connected Xero, the invoice is automatically synced to your Xero account when it is created. There is no need to manually push it later from the web dashboard.
</Callout>

<Callout variant="tip">
  Send the invoice while still on-site so you can walk the customer through the charges if they have questions. This reduces disputes and speeds up payment.
</Callout>

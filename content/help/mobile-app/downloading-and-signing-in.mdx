# Downloading and signing in to the app

The VanMan mobile app is available for iOS and Android. It gives drivers, operators, and admins access to jobs, the calendar, payments, and team chat from their phone.

<StepByStep>
  <Step title="Download the app">
    Open the **App Store** on iPhone or **Google Play** on Android and search for "VanMan". Tap **Install** to download the app to your device.

    <Callout variant="tip">
      You can also find download links from within VanMan by navigating to <UIPath path="Settings > Mobile App" /> where QR codes and direct links to both app stores are provided. Share these with your team to speed up onboarding.
    </Callout>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Open the app">
    Once installed, tap the VanMan icon on your home screen to launch the app.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Sign in">
    Enter the **email address** and **password** you use for your VanMan account. If your admin invited you via email, use the credentials from your invitation. Tap **Sign in** to access the app.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Allow notifications">
    When prompted, allow push notifications so you receive alerts for new messages, job updates, and schedule changes. You can adjust notification settings later in your device's settings.
  </Step>
</StepByStep>

<Callout variant="info">
  The mobile app syncs with the web app in real time. Any jobs, messages, or schedule changes made on the web are immediately visible on mobile, and vice versa.
</Callout>

# Managing user roles

VanMan has three user roles that control what each team member can access. Assigning the right role ensures everyone has the tools they need without exposing settings they should not change.

## Role overview

| Role | What they can do |
|------|-----------------|
| **Admin** | Full access to everything — jobs, calendar, invoicing, settings, billing, and team management. |
| **Operator** | Manage jobs, the calendar, customer communications, and invoicing. Cannot access billing or account settings. |
| **Driver** | View assigned jobs for the day, track time, navigate to addresses, use team chat, and add notes and photos to jobs. |

<Callout variant="info">
  Most moving companies set the business owner as Admin, office staff as Operators, and crew members as Drivers. You can have as many of each role as you need.
</Callout>

## Changing a team member's role

<StepByStep>
  <Step title="Open team settings">
    Navigate to <UIPath path="Settings > Team" /> to see the list of all team members and their current roles.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select the team member">
    Click the team member whose role you want to change.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Update the role">
    Select the new role from the **Role** dropdown and click **Save**. The change takes effect immediately — the team member's access is updated the next time they load any page or open the app.
  </Step>
</StepByStep>

<Callout variant="tip">
  If a driver is promoted to a scheduling role, change their role to Operator so they gain access to the calendar and job management features.
</Callout>

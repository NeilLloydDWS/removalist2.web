# Managing your subscription and billing

VanMan uses a simple all-inclusive pricing plan. You can manage every aspect of your subscription — invoices, payment methods, and billing cycle — from the billing portal.

<StepByStep>
  <Step title="Open the billing portal">
    Navigate to <UIPath path="Settings > Billing" /> and click **Manage billing**. This opens the Stripe billing portal where all your subscription details are managed.
  </Step>

  <Step title="View your invoices">
    The billing portal shows a history of all invoices including the amount, date, and payment status. Click any invoice to download a PDF for your records.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Switch billing cycle">
    You can switch between **monthly** and **annual** billing at any time. Annual billing saves 20% compared to monthly. The change takes effect at the start of your next billing period.
  </Step>
</StepByStep>

<Callout variant="info">
  VanMan has a single plan with all features included — unlimited jobs, unlimited contacts, unlimited users, Xero integration, mobile app, team chat, and customer communications. There are no feature tiers or per-user charges.
</Callout>

<Callout variant="tip">
  Switch to annual billing to save 20%. You can switch back to monthly at any time if your needs change.
</Callout>

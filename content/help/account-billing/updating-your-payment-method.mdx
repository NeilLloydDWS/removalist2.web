# Updating your payment method

You can update your payment method at any time from the billing portal. This is useful when your card expires, you want to switch to a different card, or you need to add a payment method after your free trial.

<StepByStep>
  <Step title="Open the billing portal">
    Navigate to <UIPath path="Settings > Billing" /> and click **Manage billing** to open the Stripe billing portal.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Update your payment method">
    In the billing portal, find the **Payment method** section and click **Update**. Enter your new card details — card number, expiry date, and CVC.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save the new card">
    Click **Save** to confirm. Your new card is stored securely and will be used for all future billing charges. The old card is removed automatically.
  </Step>
</StepByStep>

<Callout variant="info">
  VanMan accepts all major credit and debit cards including Visa, Mastercard, and American Express. If you need to pay by bank transfer, contact support to arrange invoice-based billing.
</Callout>

<Callout variant="warning">
  If your card on file expires or is declined, VanMan will retry the payment and send you a notification. Update your payment method promptly to avoid any interruption to your service.
</Callout>

# Understanding your free trial

Every VanMan account starts with a 14-day free trial. During the trial you have full access to all features with no restrictions and no credit card required.

## What is included

Your free trial includes everything in the VanMan plan:

- Unlimited jobs and contacts
- Unlimited users
- Calendar and scheduling
- Online booking form
- Customer SMS and email
- Team chat
- Mobile app for iOS and Android
- Xero integration

<Callout variant="info">
  No credit card is required to start your trial. You can explore every feature without entering any payment details.
</Callout>

## What happens when the trial ends

At the end of 14 days, your account is paused if no payment method has been added. Your data is preserved — nothing is deleted. To resume using VanMan, add a payment method from <UIPath path="Settings > Billing" /> and your account is reactivated immediately.

<StepByStep>
  <Step title="Add a payment method before the trial ends">
    Navigate to <UIPath path="Settings > Billing" /> and click **Manage billing**. Add a credit card to ensure a seamless transition from trial to paid subscription.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Continue uninterrupted">
    If a payment method is on file when the trial ends, your subscription begins automatically with no downtime. You are billed at the start of your first paid period.
  </Step>
</StepByStep>

<Callout variant="tip">
  Add your payment method early in the trial so you do not forget. You will not be charged until the 14-day trial period ends.
</Callout>

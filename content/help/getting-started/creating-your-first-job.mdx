# Creating your first job

Jobs are at the heart of VanMan. Each job represents a single moving booking with a customer, including their contact information, pickup and delivery addresses, and the scheduled date and truck assignment.

<Callout variant="info">
  VanMan integrates with Google Maps to provide address autocomplete. As you type a pickup or delivery address, suggestions appear based on real locations. Selecting a suggestion ensures the address is accurately geocoded, which enables travel time estimates and route links for your crew on the day.
</Callout>

<StepByStep>
  <Step title="Start a new job">
    Click the **New job** button from the top navigation bar or from the calendar view. This opens the job creation form where you will enter all the details for the booking.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter customer details">
    Fill in the customer's **name**, **phone number**, and **email address**. These details are used for sending booking confirmations, reminders, and invoices. If the customer already exists in your system, VanMan will suggest matching contacts as you type so you can link the job to an existing record.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add pickup and delivery addresses">
    Enter the **pickup address** where your crew will collect the customer's belongings and the **delivery address** where items will be taken. Use the autocomplete suggestions to select verified addresses. You can add additional stops if the job involves multiple locations by clicking **Add stop** beneath the address fields.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set the date, time, and duration">
    Choose the **job date** from the date picker and set the **start time**. Optionally, enter an estimated **duration** in hours. The duration helps VanMan display the correct block size on the calendar and prevents overlapping bookings when you have back-to-back jobs scheduled for the same truck.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Assign a truck">
    Select a truck from the **Truck** dropdown. The list shows each truck by name and colour along with its availability for the chosen date. If a truck already has a conflicting booking, it will be flagged so you can pick an available alternative. Once assigned, the job will appear on the calendar in that truck's colour.
  </Step>
</StepByStep>

Click **Save job** to confirm the booking. The job now appears on your calendar and is ready for dispatch. You can return to edit any details at any time before the job is completed.

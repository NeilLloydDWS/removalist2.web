# Understanding job statuses

Every job in VanMan follows a lifecycle represented by a series of statuses. Each status reflects where the job is in your workflow, from the initial enquiry through to the final invoice.

## The job lifecycle

Jobs progress through these statuses:

- **Enquiry** — A new lead or booking request has come in but has not been confirmed yet.
- **Quote** — You have sent the customer a price and are waiting for their response.
- **Confirmed** — The customer has accepted and the job is locked in on your calendar.
- **In Progress** — Your crew is on-site and the move is underway.
- **Completed** — The job is finished and ready to be invoiced.
- **Invoiced** — An invoice has been generated and sent to the customer.

<Callout variant="info">
  Job statuses update automatically at certain stages. For example, when you create an invoice from a completed job, the status changes to Invoiced without any manual action.
</Callout>

## Changing a status manually

Open the job and click the **Status** dropdown to move it to the next stage. You can skip stages if needed — for example, moving a job directly from Enquiry to Confirmed if no formal quote is required.

<Callout variant="tip">
  Use the status filter on the jobs list to focus on jobs at a particular stage. For example, filter by **Completed** to find all jobs ready for invoicing at the end of the week.
</Callout>

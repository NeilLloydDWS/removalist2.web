# Job history and audit trail

Every action taken on a job in VanMan is recorded in the activity log. This creates a complete audit trail that you can review at any time to understand what happened and when.

## What is logged

The activity log captures:

- **Status changes** — when the job moved from one status to the next and who made the change.
- **Edits** — any modifications to customer details, addresses, dates, truck assignments, or notes.
- **Messages** — SMS and email messages sent to the customer from the job, including delivery status.
- **Invoices** — when an invoice was created and synced to Xero.
- **Time tracking** — when a driver started and stopped the timer on the mobile app.

## Viewing the activity log

Open the job detail panel and scroll to the **Activity** section at the bottom. Each entry shows the action that was taken, the name of the team member who performed it, and a timestamp.

<Callout variant="info">
  The activity log is read-only and cannot be edited or deleted. This ensures the audit trail remains accurate and complete for every job.
</Callout>

<Callout variant="tip">
  Use the activity log to review what happened on a job if a customer queries their invoice or raises a concern. The log provides a factual record of every interaction and change.
</Callout>

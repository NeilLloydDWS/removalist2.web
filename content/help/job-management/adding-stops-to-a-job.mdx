# Adding stops to a job

Some moves involve more than one pickup or delivery location. VanMan lets you add additional stops to any job so your crew has a clear itinerary for the day.

<StepByStep>
  <Step title="Open the job for editing">
    Navigate to the job and click **Edit** to open the editing form. Scroll to the address section where the pickup and delivery addresses are listed.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add a stop">
    Click **Add stop** beneath the existing address fields. A new address field appears where you can enter the additional location. Use the Google Maps autocomplete to select a verified address.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Reorder stops if needed">
    Stops are visited in the order they appear on the job. Drag stops to rearrange them into the correct sequence for your crew's route.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save the job">
    Click **Save job** to confirm the updated itinerary. Your crew will see all stops in order when they view the job on the mobile app.
  </Step>
</StepByStep>

<Callout variant="info">
  Each stop uses Google Maps autocomplete for address entry, which ensures accurate geocoding. Your drivers can tap any address on the mobile app to open turn-by-turn navigation directly to that location.
</Callout>

<Callout variant="tip">
  Add notes to the job to give your crew context about each stop — for example, "Second stop is storage unit, customer will meet at gate."
</Callout>

# Adding notes and photos to a job

Notes and photos help you keep important information attached to each job. Use notes for special instructions and photos to document the condition of items or access requirements.

## Adding notes

<StepByStep>
  <Step title="Open the job">
    Navigate to the job from the calendar or jobs list and open the job detail panel.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter your notes">
    Scroll to the **Notes** section and type your instructions or information. Notes are free-text and can include details like access codes, parking instructions, fragile items, or anything your crew needs to know on the day.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save">
    Click **Save job** to store the notes. They are visible to all operators and to drivers viewing the job on the mobile app.
  </Step>
</StepByStep>

## Adding photos

Photos can be attached to a job from the mobile app. Drivers and operators can take photos directly from their phone and attach them to the job for documentation purposes.

<Callout variant="tip">
  Ask your crew to photograph the condition of items before loading. This creates a record that can be referenced if a damage claim arises after the move.
</Callout>

<Callout variant="info">
  All photos attached to a job are stored in the job detail panel and are accessible to everyone on your team. They remain attached to the job permanently as part of the job's record.
</Callout>

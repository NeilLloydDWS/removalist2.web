# Editing a job

You can edit any job in VanMan at any time before it is completed. Changes are saved immediately and visible to all operators and drivers in real time.

<StepByStep>
  <Step title="Open the job">
    Find the job on the calendar or in the jobs list and click it to open the job detail panel. Click **Edit** to switch to editing mode.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Update the details you need to change">
    You can modify any field on the job including:

    - **Customer details** — name, phone number, email address
    - **Addresses** — pickup, delivery, and any additional stops
    - **Scheduling** — date, start time, and estimated duration
    - **Truck assignment** — switch to a different truck
    - **Notes** — add or update special instructions
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save your changes">
    Click **Save job** to confirm the updates. The calendar and job list update immediately to reflect the new details.
  </Step>
</StepByStep>

<Callout variant="warning">
  If you change the truck or date on a job, check the calendar first to make sure the new truck is available at the new time. VanMan will warn you if the change creates a scheduling conflict.
</Callout>

<Callout variant="tip">
  All changes to a job are recorded in the activity log with a timestamp and the name of the user who made the change. You can view this log at the bottom of the job detail panel.
</Callout>

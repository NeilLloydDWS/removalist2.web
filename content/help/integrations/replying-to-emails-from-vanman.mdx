# Replying to emails from VanMan

When an email is captured against a job, you can reply to it directly from VanMan. Your reply is sent from your connected email account and threaded into the existing conversation.

<StepByStep>
  <Step title="Open the job">
    Navigate to the job that has the email you want to reply to. Open the **Messages** tab to see all communications for this job.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Find the email">
    Scroll to the email you want to respond to. Captured emails are shown with a mail icon and include the sender, subject, and body preview.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click Reply">
    Click the **Reply** button on the email. The reply drawer will open with the original email quoted below your compose area.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Write and send">
    Type your response and click **Send**. VanMan sends the reply from your connected email address (Gmail or Outlook). The customer sees it as a normal email reply from you — not from VanMan.

    <Callout variant="info">
      Your reply is threaded into the original email conversation. The customer's email client will show it as part of the same thread.
    </Callout>
  </Step>
</StepByStep>

Your sent reply also appears on the job's Messages tab so your team can see the full conversation.

<Callout variant="tip">
  The reply includes the job number in the subject line automatically. This ensures any further replies from the customer are also captured against the job.
</Callout>

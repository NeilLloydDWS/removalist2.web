# Connecting Outlook

Connect your Microsoft 365 or Outlook account to VanMan and emails mentioning job numbers will be automatically captured against the right job — just like the Gmail integration.

<StepByStep>
  <Step title="Open integration settings">
    Navigate to <UIPath path="Settings > Integrations" />. Find the **Outlook** card.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Start the connection">
    Click the **Connect** button. VanMan will redirect you to Microsoft's sign-in page where you can choose which account to connect.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Authorise VanMan">
    Microsoft will ask you to grant VanMan permission to read and send emails. Review the permissions and click **Accept**. VanMan needs these permissions to capture emails and enable replies from within the app.

    <Callout variant="info">
      VanMan only processes emails that contain job numbers. Your credentials are encrypted with AES-256 and stored securely.
    </Callout>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Confirm the connection">
    You will be redirected back to VanMan. The Outlook card will show your connected email address and a green status indicator. Email capture begins immediately.
  </Step>
</StepByStep>

To disconnect Outlook, click the **Disconnect** button on the Outlook card. Previously captured emails remain on their jobs.

<Callout variant="tip">
  The Outlook integration works identically to Gmail. Emails are matched by job number, attachments are stored, and you can reply from inside VanMan.
</Callout>

# Viewing live truck locations

The live map shows the real-time GPS position of every truck with the VanMan mobile app running. Use it to see where your fleet is, check on progress, and coordinate your operation.

<StepByStep>
  <Step title="Open the live map">
    Navigate to <UIPath path="Trucks > Live Map" />. The map loads showing all trucks that are currently sharing their location.
  </Step>

  <Step title="View truck positions">
    Each truck appears as a colour-coded marker on the map. The marker colour matches the truck's colour from your fleet settings. Hover over a marker to see the truck name, driver, speed, and last updated time.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Zoom and navigate">
    Use the map controls to zoom in on specific areas. The map automatically centres on your fleet. If a truck is moving, the marker updates its position in real time without needing to refresh the page.
  </Step>
</StepByStep>

<Callout variant="info">
  Trucks only appear on the live map when the driver has the VanMan mobile app open and location permissions granted. If a truck is not visible, the driver may have closed the app or disabled location services.
</Callout>

<Callout variant="tip">
  Use the live map alongside the Day Plan for a complete operational view. The Day Plan adds job routes and calendar context to the live truck positions.
</Callout>

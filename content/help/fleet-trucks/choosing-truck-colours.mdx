# Choosing truck colours for the calendar

Each truck in VanMan is assigned a colour that is used throughout the app — on the calendar, in job assignments, and in fleet views. Choosing distinct colours makes it easy to see which truck is assigned to each job at a glance.

## Selecting a colour

Navigate to <UIPath path="Settings > Trucks" /> and click the truck you want to update. The colour field offers two options:

- **Preset palette** — eight pre-selected colours (blue, red, green, orange, purple, pink, cyan, and amber) designed to be visually distinct from each other.
- **Custom hex code** — click the colour picker to choose any colour or enter a specific hex value to match your company branding.

<Callout variant="tip">
  Choose contrasting colours for trucks that are frequently scheduled on the same days. If two trucks have similar colours, it can be hard to tell them apart on a busy calendar.
</Callout>

## Where colours appear

Your truck colour is displayed:

- As the **lane colour** on the weekly calendar view
- On **job blocks** within that truck's lane
- In the **truck dropdown** when assigning a truck to a job
- On the **mobile app** when drivers view their assigned jobs

<Callout variant="info">
  You can change a truck's colour at any time. The calendar and all active jobs update immediately to reflect the new colour. Completed jobs in the history retain the colour they had at the time.
</Callout>

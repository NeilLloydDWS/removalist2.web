# Assigning a default team leader

Each truck in VanMan can have a default team leader. When the truck is assigned to a job, the team leader is automatically included as part of the crew. This saves time during scheduling and ensures your lead driver is always paired with the right vehicle.

<StepByStep>
  <Step title="Open truck settings">
    Navigate to <UIPath path="Settings > Trucks" /> and click the truck you want to update.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select a team leader">
    Click the **Team leader** dropdown and choose a team member from the list. The dropdown shows all active team members along with their role.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save">
    Click **Save** to set the default. From now on, whenever this truck is assigned to a job, the selected team leader is automatically added to the crew.
  </Step>
</StepByStep>

<Callout variant="info">
  The default team leader is a convenience setting. You can override it on any individual job by changing the crew assignment manually. Removing or changing the default does not affect jobs that have already been scheduled.
</Callout>

<Callout variant="tip">
  If a team leader is on leave, you can temporarily change the default to another crew member. Swap it back when they return so future jobs are assigned correctly.
</Callout>

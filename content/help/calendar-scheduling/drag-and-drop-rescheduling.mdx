# Drag-and-drop rescheduling

VanMan lets you reschedule jobs by dragging them directly on the calendar. You can move a job to a different day, a different truck, or both in a single action.

<StepByStep>
  <Step title="Click and hold a job block">
    On the weekly calendar view, click and hold the job block you want to move. The block will lift slightly to indicate it is ready to be dragged.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Drag to the new position">
    Move the job block to the desired day and truck lane. As you drag, the calendar highlights valid drop zones. Release the block to confirm the new position.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Confirm the change">
    The job is rescheduled immediately. The new date and truck assignment are saved automatically and the change is visible to all operators and drivers in real time.
  </Step>
</StepByStep>

<Callout variant="info">
  Drag-and-drop works on the weekly view. If a truck already has a job at the target time, VanMan will warn you about the conflict before completing the move.
</Callout>

## What gets updated

When you drag a job to a new position, VanMan updates the **job date**, **start time**, and **truck assignment** based on where you drop it. All other job details — customer information, addresses, notes, and charges — remain unchanged.

<Callout variant="tip">
  Drag-and-drop is the fastest way to handle last-minute schedule changes. Instead of opening the job and editing fields manually, you can rearrange your entire day in seconds.
</Callout>

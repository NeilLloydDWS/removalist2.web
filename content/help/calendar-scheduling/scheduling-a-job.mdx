# Scheduling a job on the calendar

You can create a new job from anywhere in VanMan, and it will automatically appear on the calendar once a date and truck are assigned.

<StepByStep>
  <Step title="Open the new job form">
    Click the **New job** button in the top navigation bar. You can also click directly on an empty time slot in the calendar to pre-fill the date, time, and truck for that slot.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter customer and address details">
    Fill in the customer's **name**, **phone number**, and **email address**. Then enter the **pickup address** and **delivery address** using the autocomplete suggestions powered by Google Maps.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set the date and time">
    Choose the **job date** from the date picker and set the **start time**. Enter an estimated **duration** in hours so the calendar displays the correct block size for the job.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Assign a truck">
    Select a truck from the **Truck** dropdown. The list shows each truck's name, colour, and availability for the chosen date. Trucks with conflicting bookings are flagged so you can choose an available alternative.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save the job">
    Click **Save job** to confirm. The job immediately appears on the calendar in the assigned truck's colour and is visible to all operators and drivers in real time.
  </Step>
</StepByStep>

<Callout variant="tip">
  If you click on an empty slot in the calendar to start a new job, the date, time, and truck are pre-filled automatically. This is the fastest way to schedule when you are already looking at the calendar.
</Callout>

# Using workflow templates

VanMan includes pre-built workflow templates for common removalist scenarios. Templates give you a working workflow in seconds that you can customise to fit your process.

<StepByStep>
  <Step title="Open the templates">
    Navigate to <UIPath path="Settings > Workflows" /> and click the **Templates** tab. You will see a list of available templates with descriptions of what each one does.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose a template">
    Browse the available templates. Common examples include:
    - **Booking confirmation** — sends an SMS when a booking is confirmed
    - **Job completion follow-up** — sends a thank-you email and creates an invoice when a job is completed
    - **New booking notification** — alerts the operations manager when a new booking arrives
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create from template">
    Click **Use Template** on the one you want. VanMan creates a new workflow pre-configured with the trigger, conditions, and actions from the template.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Customise and activate">
    Open the new workflow in the editor. Adjust the message templates, timing, or conditions to match your business. Then toggle the **Active** switch to turn it on.

    <Callout variant="tip">
      Templates are a starting point. You can add, remove, or rearrange nodes after creating a workflow from a template.
    </Callout>
  </Step>
</StepByStep>

<Callout variant="info">
  Templates do not affect your existing workflows. Each time you use a template, a new independent workflow is created.
</Callout>

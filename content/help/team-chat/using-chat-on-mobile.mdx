# Using chat on mobile

Team chat is fully available on the VanMan mobile app for both iOS and Android. You can start conversations, send messages, and stay connected with your team from anywhere.

## Accessing chat

Tap the **Chat** tab in the mobile app's navigation bar to open your conversations list. You see the same conversations as on the web — everything syncs in real time between devices.

## The conversation list

The chat list shows all your conversations sorted by most recent activity. Each entry displays the contact's name, a preview of the last message, a relative timestamp (such as "5m" or "2h"), and an unread badge if there are new messages.

## Sending messages on mobile

Tap a conversation to open it. Type your message in the input field at the bottom of the screen and tap **Send**. The keyboard automatically adjusts so you can see the conversation while typing.

<Callout variant="info">
  Team chat works offline. If you lose signal, messages are queued on your device and sent automatically when your connection is restored. You will not lose any messages even in areas with patchy coverage.
</Callout>

<Callout variant="tip">
  Use the staff directory within the new conversation screen to quickly find the right person. Team members are listed by name and role, making it easy to contact drivers, operators, or admins.
</Callout>

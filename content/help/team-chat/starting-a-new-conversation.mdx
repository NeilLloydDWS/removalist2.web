# Starting a new conversation

VanMan includes built-in team chat so you can message your crew directly without switching to external apps. Conversations are one-to-one direct messages between team members.

<StepByStep>
  <Step title="Open the chat section">
    Navigate to <UIPath path="Chat" /> from the main navigation. The chat screen shows your existing conversations listed by most recent activity.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Start a new conversation">
    Tap the **New conversation** button. A list of your team members appears showing each person's name and role.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select a team member">
    Tap the person you want to message. A new conversation is created and the message input field opens so you can start typing immediately.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Send your first message">
    Type your message and tap **Send**. The conversation now appears in both your chat list and the other person's chat list.
  </Step>
</StepByStep>

<Callout variant="info">
  Conversations persist across sessions. Once you start a conversation with someone, it stays in your chat list so you can pick up where you left off at any time.
</Callout>

<Callout variant="tip">
  Use team chat to coordinate with drivers on the day of a move — quick questions like "Are you on your way?" or "Customer is running late" are faster to handle via chat than a phone call.
</Callout>

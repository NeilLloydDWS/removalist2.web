# Push notifications for messages

VanMan sends push notifications to your iOS or Android device when you receive a new chat message. This ensures you see important messages from your team even when you are not actively looking at the app.

## What you receive

When a team member sends you a message, a push notification appears on your device showing:

- **The sender's name** — so you know who the message is from.
- **A message preview** — the first part of the message text so you can decide whether to respond immediately.

Tap the notification to open the conversation directly in the VanMan mobile app.

<Callout variant="info">
  VanMan is smart about notifications. If you have recently opened a conversation (within the last 30 seconds), you will not receive a push notification for new messages in that conversation. This prevents unnecessary alerts when you are already actively chatting.
</Callout>

## Managing notifications

Push notifications require that you allow notifications for VanMan in your device settings. If you are not receiving notifications:

1. Open your device's **Settings** app.
2. Navigate to **Notifications** and find **VanMan**.
3. Ensure notifications are **enabled** and set to show on the lock screen and as banners.

<Callout variant="tip">
  Keep push notifications enabled so you do not miss time-sensitive messages from your crew on move days. You can use your device's Do Not Disturb mode to silence all notifications during off hours.
</Callout>

# Read status and unread counts

VanMan tracks which messages you have read and displays unread counts so you can quickly see which conversations need your attention.

## Unread badges

When a team member sends you a message that you have not yet read, an **unread count badge** appears next to that conversation in the chat list. The badge shows the number of new messages waiting. Opening the conversation automatically marks all messages as read and the badge disappears.

<Callout variant="info">
  A conversation is marked as read the moment you open it. You do not need to scroll through the messages — simply viewing the conversation is enough to clear the unread count.
</Callout>

## How read tracking works

VanMan records the last time you opened each conversation. Any messages received after that timestamp are counted as unread. This tracking works independently on web and mobile — opening a conversation on your phone also clears the unread count on the web, and vice versa.

## Staying on top of messages

The chat list is sorted by most recent activity. Conversations with new messages rise to the top of the list so you do not need to scroll to find them.

<Callout variant="tip">
  Check your chat list at the start and end of each day to ensure you have not missed any messages from your team. Unread badges make it easy to spot conversations that need a response.
</Callout>

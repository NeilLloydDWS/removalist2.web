# Generating a quote from materials

After adding materials to a job, you can generate a customer-facing quote that shows the breakdown of costs. This helps customers understand what they are paying for and builds trust.

<StepByStep>
  <Step title="Add materials to the job">
    Make sure you have added all relevant materials and costs to the job's **Costing** tab. The quote will be generated from these items.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Generate the quote">
    Click the **Generate Quote** button on the Costing tab. VanMan creates a quote document with all materials listed as line items, including quantities, unit prices, and totals.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review and customise">
    Preview the quote before sending. You can add or remove line items, adjust prices, and add notes or terms. The total including GST is recalculated automatically.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Send to the customer">
    Send the quote to the customer via email or SMS. The customer receives a professional document showing the full cost breakdown.

    <Callout variant="tip">
      Once the customer accepts the quote, you can convert it to an invoice with one click. All line items carry over — no re-entry needed.
    </Callout>
  </Step>
</StepByStep>

<Callout variant="info">
  Quotes are stored against the job alongside invoices and other documents. You can view and resend them at any time from the job detail page.
</Callout>

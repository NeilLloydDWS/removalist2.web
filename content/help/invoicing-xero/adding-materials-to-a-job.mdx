# Adding materials to a job

Once your material catalog is set up, you can add materials to individual jobs. This gives you accurate cost tracking and feeds into quotes and invoices.

<StepByStep>
  <Step title="Open the job">
    Navigate to the job you want to add materials to and open the **Costing** tab.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add a material">
    Click **Add Material**. Search for the material by name or browse by category. Select the material you want to add.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set the quantity">
    Enter the quantity — for example, 10 boxes, 3 hours of labour, or 2 rolls of bubble wrap. The total cost is calculated automatically based on the unit price from your catalog.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review the job costs">
    The Costing tab shows all materials added to the job with quantities, unit prices, and line totals. The overall job cost including GST is calculated at the bottom.

    <Callout variant="tip">
      You can adjust the unit price on individual job materials without changing the catalog price. This is useful for one-off discounts or custom pricing.
    </Callout>
  </Step>
</StepByStep>

When you create an invoice from this job, the materials are automatically included as line items.

<Callout variant="info">
  Materials can be added at any stage of the job — from quoting through to completion. Add them early for accurate quotes, or track them as you go.
</Callout>

# Understanding the booking pipeline

The booking pipeline in VanMan tracks every enquiry through a series of stages. This helps you see where each lead stands and ensures no enquiry falls through the cracks.

## Pipeline stages

Each booking moves through these stages:

- **New** — A fresh enquiry has been submitted through the booking form. No one has responded yet.
- **Contacted** — You have reached out to the customer by phone, email, or SMS.
- **Quoted** — You have sent the customer a price for their move and are awaiting their decision.
- **Confirmed** — The customer has accepted your quote and the booking is locked in. The enquiry is ready to be converted to a job.
- **Declined** — The enquiry did not proceed. This could be because the customer chose another company, cancelled their move, or you were unable to accommodate the request.

<Callout variant="info">
  You can move an enquiry to any stage at any time. Stages are not locked in sequence — if a customer calls back after being quoted and confirms immediately, you can skip straight to Confirmed.
</Callout>

## Using the pipeline to manage your workload

Filter the bookings list by stage to focus on what needs attention. For example, filter by **New** at the start of each day to see which enquiries need a first response, or filter by **Quoted** to follow up on outstanding quotes.

<Callout variant="tip">
  Track your conversion rate from New to Confirmed using the analytics dashboard. This shows you how effectively you are turning enquiries into paying jobs and where leads are dropping off.
</Callout>

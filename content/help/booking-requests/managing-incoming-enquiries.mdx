# Managing incoming enquiries

When a customer submits a booking request through your online form, it appears in VanMan as a new enquiry. You can review the details, contact the customer, and move the enquiry through your pipeline.

<StepByStep>
  <Step title="View new enquiries">
    Navigate to <UIPath path="Bookings" /> to see all incoming enquiries. New submissions appear at the top of the list with a **New** status badge. Each entry shows the customer name, move date, and addresses.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review the details">
    Click an enquiry to open the full details including all form fields the customer submitted — addresses, preferred dates, contact information, and any notes they included.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Contact the customer">
    Use the customer's phone number or email to follow up. Once you have contacted them, update the status to **Contacted** so your team knows this lead is being handled.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Send a quote or confirm">
    After discussing the details with the customer, move the enquiry to **Quoted** if you have sent pricing, or directly to **Confirmed** if the customer is ready to book.
  </Step>
</StepByStep>

<Callout variant="info">
  VanMan sends you an email notification each time a new enquiry is submitted so you can respond quickly. Fast response times help convert more leads into confirmed jobs.
</Callout>

<Callout variant="tip">
  Check your bookings dashboard at the start of each day to catch any overnight enquiries. Responding within a few hours gives you the best chance of winning the job.
</Callout>

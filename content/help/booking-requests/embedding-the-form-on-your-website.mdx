# Embedding the booking form on your website

Once your booking form is active, you can embed it directly on your company website so customers can submit enquiries without leaving your site.

<StepByStep>
  <Step title="Copy the embed code">
    Navigate to <UIPath path="Settings > Booking Form" /> and find the **Embed** section. Click **Copy code** to copy the embed snippet to your clipboard.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add the code to your website">
    Paste the embed code into the HTML of the page where you want the booking form to appear. Place it inside the body of the page, typically within a container or section element. The form loads as an iframe that matches the width of its container.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Publish and test">
    Publish the changes to your website and visit the page to confirm the form displays correctly. Submit a test enquiry to verify it arrives in your VanMan dashboard.
  </Step>
</StepByStep>

<Callout variant="tip">
  Place the booking form on a dedicated "Get a Quote" or "Book a Move" page on your website. Link to this page from your homepage and navigation menu so customers can find it easily.
</Callout>

## Sharing a direct link

If you prefer not to embed the form, you can share the booking form as a standalone link. Copy the **Direct link** from <UIPath path="Settings > Booking Form" /> and share it via email, social media, or your Google Business listing. The link opens the form in a clean, mobile-friendly page hosted by VanMan.

<Callout variant="info">
  The embedded form and the direct link use the same form configuration. Any changes you make to fields or settings apply to both automatically.
</Callout>

# Sending an SMS from a job

VanMan lets you send SMS messages to customers directly from the job detail page. Messages are logged against the job so your entire team can see the conversation history.

<StepByStep>
  <Step title="Open the job">
    Navigate to the job from the calendar or the jobs list and open the job detail panel.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click Send SMS">
    Click the **Send SMS** button in the messages section. A message composer opens where you can write your message or select a template.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Write or select a template">
    Type your message in the text field, or click **Use template** to choose from your saved templates. If you select a template, smart variables like the customer's name and move date are filled in automatically.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Send the message">
    Review the message and click **Send**. The SMS is delivered to the customer's phone number on file. A record of the message appears in the job's message history with a delivery status indicator.
  </Step>
</StepByStep>

<Callout variant="info">
  The customer's phone number is pulled from the job's customer details. If no phone number is set, you will be prompted to add one before sending.
</Callout>

<Callout variant="tip">
  Use SMS for time-sensitive messages like booking confirmations and day-of-move updates. SMS has higher open rates than email, so important information is more likely to be seen quickly.
</Callout>

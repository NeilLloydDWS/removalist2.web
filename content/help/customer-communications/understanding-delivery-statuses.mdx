# Understanding delivery statuses

Every message you send from VanMan — whether SMS or email — is tracked with a delivery status so you know whether the customer received it.

## Status types

Messages progress through these statuses:

- **Queued** — The message has been submitted and is waiting to be sent.
- **Sent** — The message has left VanMan's servers and is in transit.
- **Delivered** — The message has been successfully delivered to the customer's phone or email inbox.
- **Opened** — The customer has opened the email. This status is only available for email messages.
- **Failed** — The message could not be delivered. This can happen if the phone number is invalid or the email address does not exist.
- **Bounced** — The email was rejected by the recipient's mail server, typically due to an invalid or full mailbox.

<Callout variant="info">
  Delivery statuses update automatically as the message progresses. You do not need to refresh the page — the status badge on the message updates in real time.
</Callout>

## Viewing message statuses

Open the job and scroll to the **Messages** section. Each message shows a status badge next to the timestamp. Click on a message to see the full delivery timeline including when it was sent, delivered, and opened.

<Callout variant="tip">
  If a message shows as **Failed**, check the customer's phone number or email address for typos. Correct the details on the job and resend the message.
</Callout>

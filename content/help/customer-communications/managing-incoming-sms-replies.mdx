# Managing incoming SMS replies

VanMan supports two-way SMS. When a customer replies to an SMS you sent, their response appears directly in the job's message history so your entire team can see the conversation.

## How incoming replies work

When a customer replies to an SMS sent from VanMan, the reply is automatically matched to the correct job and displayed in the **Messages** section of the job detail panel. The message shows the customer's name, the reply text, and the time it was received.

<Callout variant="info">
  Incoming replies are matched based on the customer's phone number. If the same customer has multiple jobs, the reply is linked to the most recent active job.
</Callout>

## Responding to a reply

<StepByStep>
  <Step title="View the reply">
    Open the job and scroll to the **Messages** section. Customer replies appear in the conversation thread alongside your sent messages, creating a clear back-and-forth dialogue.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Send a follow-up">
    Click **Send SMS** to compose a response. The conversation thread makes it easy to see the full context of the discussion before replying.
  </Step>
</StepByStep>

<Callout variant="tip">
  Check your jobs dashboard regularly for new customer replies, especially in the days leading up to a move. Customers often reply with last-minute changes or questions that need a quick response.
</Callout>

## Team visibility

All incoming replies are visible to every team member with access to the job. This means if you are unavailable, a colleague can pick up the conversation and respond on your behalf without missing any context.
